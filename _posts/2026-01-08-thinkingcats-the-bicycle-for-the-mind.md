## 4. The Reasoning Flow: Parallel Dialectics
ThinkingCats doesn't just "chat." It reasons through a structured sequence that strips away ego and focuses on bedrock reality.

1.  **Blue Hat (Process):** The system defines the problem and sets the focus.
2.  **White Hat (Data):** Experts contribute objective facts. No ego.
3.  **Green Hat (Creativity):** The roundtable generates provocations and new ideas.
4.  **Yellow/Black Hat (Evaluation):** A rigorous analysis of value versus risk—finding flaws before they find you.
5.  **Red Hat (Intuition):** A final gut-check from the personas.
6.  **Crystallization:** The logic is distilled into the Artifact.
